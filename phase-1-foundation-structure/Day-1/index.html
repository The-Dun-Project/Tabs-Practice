Day 1 · Foundation

The craft of building things

Every interface you have ever used was written line by line by someone who once knew nothing. The only difference between you and them is the number of hours spent typing, breaking things, and figuring out why. You are in that process now.

Day 3 · Motion

Motion earns its place

Animation is not decoration. It is information — it tells the user where they are, where something came from, and where it went. A good animation makes the interface feel alive. A bad one just makes people wait.

Day 5 · Transition

The Fade is a conversation

When content fades out before new content fades in, you are telling the user the old thing is leaving and something new is arriving. That small moment of acknowledgment is what makes a UI feel considered rather than cheap.
